Introduction and Learning Objectives

In this project I provide narration for Adobe Premiere Pro video editing application of an online learning module. This will give student an overview of capabilities of Adobe Premiere Pro and how to applies those functionalities to create great videos.

Learning Objectives

Students will learn how to use Adobe Premiere Pro to create, edit, and produce videos and films.

Techniques

  • The intro and outro fades in and fades out over a two second span
  • The music and captured audio play simultaneously
  • Applied a vocal enhancer to the captured audio and background music

Reflection

I had no experience with digital audio editing prior this project. I think my audio does a great job of convey the necessary information in an engaging way and I feel great about the end result of my audio. In a way, I feel like I was giving a lecture in a classroom environment. This is powerful since digital audio can achieve the result of an lecture in an education setting and can provide teaching to more students due to accessibility.
